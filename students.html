Welcome!

We are currently accepting applications for the first batch of students.

Requirements

Our requirements are very flexible. Our program aims to teach refugees, but anybody can join.
We accept both male and female. The IT industry is very welcoming for women, we highly encourage them to join our program.
You will take elearning courses only available in english and the communication will be made mostly in english. You need good english skills.
You will learn on a computer to build website and webapplications. You'll need a minimum knowledge to use and operate a computer.
If you're not sure about your skills, just get in touch with us, we'll figure this out together.
We provide you with a laptop and a place where you can work and have access to internet.

How much work?

We will give you access to various code camps, which last a few months. You will need to work a few hours per day on this program, depending on your progress.
Every week, you need to attend a meeting with a tutor and other students where you'll discuss about the ups and downs of your work. You'll also have projects to build with the collaboration of other students.
We'll bring you technical knowledge, but also social experience.
