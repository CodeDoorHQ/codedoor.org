Who we are

Codedoor is a non-profit organisation compassionately preparing and educating refugees to become software developers.

  • 50 million people in the world today have been forcefully displaced from their home ... only 20% are in school. We have 21st century educational technology at our fingertips. It's time we start asking: How can we let refugees thrive in the internet era?

    Karan Dehghani Co-founder and entrepreneur

  • I always wanted to contribute my skills as a developer to foster education. CodeDoor is an awesome way to leverage technology providing eduction to those in need.

    Nicolas Ritouet Co-founder and software craftsman
