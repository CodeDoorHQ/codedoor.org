Our mission

We are a non-profit organisation compassionately preparing and educating refugees to become software developers by providing them with tutors, material and environment to learn efficiently.
This education will support their sustainable integration into this new society and prepare them when they will be able to return home.

Our proposal

The approach we adopted to reach our goal
1.

Setup working environment.

To be efficient, the students will need a quiet and comfortable workspace with internet, electricity, some support and decent devices. With the help of our partners, we will be able to create these settings in many big German cities.

2.

Define curriculum.

Being IT professional ourselves, we know what knowledge are required to be a demanded developer. We built a program of curated courses with content from the best elearning platform. Because the best way to learn is by doing, we also offer students to build different projects during the program.

3.

Create a community.

Tutors and students will need to work closely together to learn from each other. We are building a platform for students to match with the perfect tutor and to communicate efficiently and frequently.
